About Me

My name is Semih Can Aktepe. I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Psychology in Philipps-University of Marburg. I work as a research assistant at Psychological Methods Lab (PI: Prof. Daniel Heck). My research focuses on cognitive and statistical modeling of truth judgments.
